## New to Monero?

Zero to donation in five minutes with crypto, 10-15 from fiat.

### Already have crypto (fastest)

**1.** Install [Cake Wallet](https://cakewallet.com/) (iOS/Android). No account, no email, no KYC.

**2.** Create a Monero wallet. Write down the seed phrase. This is your only backup.

**3.** Tap the exchange icon. Swap BTC, ETH, or LTC to XMR inside the app. Built-in swaps via ChangeNow/Trocador. Completes in 10-30 minutes.

**4.** Tap Send. Paste the donation address. Confirm.

### Starting from fiat (USD, EUR, GBP)

**1.** Install [Cake Wallet](https://cakewallet.com/). Create a Monero wallet. Write down the seed phrase.

**2.** Buy XMR inside Cake Wallet with a debit card or bank transfer (Onramper, DFX). Some providers require ID for fiat purchases.

**3.** Tap Send. Paste the donation address. Confirm.

*No KYC path:* Buy Bitcoin from [Bisq](https://bisq.network/) or an ATM, send to Cake Wallet, swap BTC to XMR in-app. Slower, no identity trail.

### Desktop (maximum privacy)

**1.** Download [Feather Wallet](https://featherwallet.org/) (Linux, Windows, macOS, Tails). Connects through Tor by default.

**2.** Create a wallet. Write down the seed phrase.

**3.** Get XMR: swap from another coin via [Trocador](https://trocador.app/) (no account), receive from someone, or mine it.

**4.** Send to the donation address. Feather over Tor means your IP never touches the transaction.
